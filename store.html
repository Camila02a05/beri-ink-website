Why Temporary Tattoos?

Temporary tattoos are perfect for testing out tattoo placement and size, special events and photoshoots, trying different design styles, gifting someone who loves tattoos, teenagers who are not yet old enough for permanent tattoos, and those who love the aesthetic but prefer temporary art.

All designs feature the same delicate, fine line aesthetic as my permanent work, giving you a true preview of what your tattoo could look like.

How to Apply

1

Prepare skin

Clean and dry the area; shave if needed.

2

Cut out design and remove film

Cut around the design as close as 1/26 inches (1mm) to the edge of the tattoo, for a more natural look

3

Apply & Press

Place tattoo face down on skin and press firmly

4

Wet & Wait

Wet the backing paper thoroughly using paper towel/cotton pad/cloth and wait 20 seconds or until the paper slides off

5

Peel Away

Gently peel away the paper backing to reveal your tattoo

6

Enjoy!

Your temporary tattoo will last 1-7 days with proper care
